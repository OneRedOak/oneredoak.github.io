How-To / A Step-by-Step Video & Photo Guide

  • Video 1

    A short video providing a demonstration of how to use the DOE Industries Ink proofing Kit / Quickpeek.

  • Step 1

    With Ink Knife press ink into holes in the Ink Gauge and scrape off excess ink on both sides of Ink Gauge.

  • Step 2

    Use Plunger to push the ink out of both holes in the Ink Gauge onto the Roller surface.

  • Step 3

    Ink up the Stainless Steel proofing pad making sure the whole surface is covered and that the ink is dispersed evenly a crossed the pad.

  • Step 4

    Use roller and apply pressure to transfer ink onto the desired substrate making sure the roller makes one revolution.

  • Step 5

    Ink roller up again on Stainless Steel pad using the ink that is on the pad (do not add more ink) and repeat step 4. Make 3 to 4 rollouts.

  • Step 6

    Clean Stainless Steel pad, roller, Ink Gauge, and Plunger.
